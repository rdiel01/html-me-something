Ryan Diel's top 10 Boardgames

Hi! I'm Ryan Diel and I love boardgames. I enjoy all kinds of different games and enjoy introducing them to people. There are so many different types of games that it can be very overwhelming to know where to start. This is my personal top ten list of boardgames. My goal is to help you find a game you may like by provide some insight into these games and explaining why I like them so much. Since there are so many different types of games, I have split this list into two different top ten lists. The first list will be my top introduction games, often refered to as gateway games. These games have a combination of attributes that make them easier to approach and therefore great for introducing people to the joy of hobby boardgaming. My second list consists of heavier games. These games can seem intimidating at first, but are still a blast. Please note that this list is my own opinion and I may have missed a great game. I made this website for a class and didn't have a lot of time to create my lists. I hope you enjoy these games as much as I do!
